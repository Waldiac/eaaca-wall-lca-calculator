3. Methodology / Disclaimer

This prototype calculates indicative results for wall assemblies. GWP values are assumed as kg CO₂e per m³ material. The result per m² wall area is calculated by multiplying the material value by the layer thickness in metres.

U-value calculation uses: U = 1 / (Rsi + Σ d/λ + Rse). For this first prototype, Rsi = 0.13 m²K/W and Rse = 0.04 m²K/W are used for external walls.

Module D is shown separately and is not automatically included in the A-C result. This tool does not replace a verified EPD according to EN 15804 or a project-specific building LCA according to EN 15978.
